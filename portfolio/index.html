About

Myself

Zack Yang is a designer, illustrator and photographer.
He understands that creating good designs require attention to detail, constant user testings and communication.
He has worked with several streetwear companies including, Insanity Co., Parkrabbit.
Zack intends to incoporate all the design processes and philosophies learned from past experience into his future designs.
During his free time, he is also a coffee lover and a technology enthusiast.
