Magazine Design Project Analysis #1

  • Final Design:

    Process:

    Font and color selection:

    I chose a darker color theme. With the help of yellow and red to high light and give contrast to the overall design.

    Image selection:

    I selected images that have a strong focus point inside. The candle in the first and second image draws attention to the audience and gives a impact in the overall design.

    Layout Design:

    Using Adobe InDesign, I created a grid of 16 rows and 12 columns, with 3 pica margins on every side.

    • Project Reflection

    During this project, I learned the basics of magazine design with tools like Adobe Indesign and Photoshop. From image selection to font selection, every step is important and would affect the outcome of the overall design. Learning how different color combination and font choices would change the mood of the magazine made me more careful in the future when different design requires specific tone. I also gained lots of experience with Indesign and Photoshop during this project. Reflecting on one thing that I would change about this magazine is the font choice, I would have done more research and select other fonts that are mroe unique and suits the mood of this particular magazine.

Movie Poster Project Analysis #2

  • Final Design:

    Process:

    Designer and Movie selection:

    Designer: Joseph Müller-Brockmann in the Swiss/International Design Movement.
    Movie Selection: Taxi Driver (1976)

    Joseph Müller-Brockmann design principle focuses on the core message, clean and clear visual and grid for text.
    His work also represents a feeling of movement. The main element in the movie was the taxi, so we decided to incorporate that element inside our poster design.

    Font and color selection:

    Font selection was an easier decision, Joseph Müller-Brockmann uses sans-serif fonts. Mainly Universe and Helvetica. So the fonts we chose was Open Sans for both headings and sub headings.

    Image selection:

    Using Adobe Photoshop, we deleted the background from the taxi, then we added a black & white filter along with posterized effect to give the overall image a vintage and further separate the layers of color.
    Later, we used the brush tool to clean up the reflection and the white mark on the ground.

    Layout Design:

    With the help of grid, we rotated the taxi and lined up the text.
    We also added some thin and thick line near the wheel of the taxi to create a feeling of movement.

    Project Reflection

    During this project, my partner and I sometimes has some disagreement. We both learned to listen and communicate our idea to each other then select which one suits the project better. Something that I would change about this movie poster is to seperate the font weight and the font family of the headings and the subheadings. Right now there isn't enough difference between them so words blend together, the result is a bland poster.
