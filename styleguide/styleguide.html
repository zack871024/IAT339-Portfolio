About myself

  • Zack Yang is a designer, illustrator and photographer in Vancouver. He understands that creating good designs require attention to detail, constant user testings and communication. He has worked with several streetwear companies including, Insanity Co., Parkrabbit. Zack intends to incoporate all the design processes and philosophies learned from past experience into his future designs. During his free time, he is also a coffee lover and a technology enthusiast.

Project Analysis #1

  • Process:

    Font and color selection:

    I chose a darker color theme. With the help of yellow and red to high light and give contrast to the overall design.

    Image selection:

    I selected images that have a strong focus point inside. The candle in the first and second image draws attention to the audience and gives a impact in the overall design.

    Layout Design:

    Using Adobe InDesign, I created a grid of 16 rows and 12 columns, with 3 pica margins on every side.
